2023 Movie Rankings

My ranking of the movies I have seen in 2023

1. Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

Man, this movie was amazing. The animation and plot was impressive. I can't wait for Beyond. I just hope Beyond will be made with better working conditions.

2. Dungeons & Dragons: Honor Among Thieves

Fun movie, fun cast, great representation of the game.

3. The Super Mario Bros. Movie

Fun film. Jack Black as Bowser was amazing. The nihilistic Luma was fun. My favorite moment was the Double Rider Kick at the end by Mario and Luigi.

4. Five Nights at Freddy's

Okay film. Not bad, perfectly fine use of its source materials. Matthew Lillard's William Afton was fun, despite not having much screentime.
